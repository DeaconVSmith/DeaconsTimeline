Bud Not Buddy

A Timeline by Deacon Smith of the novel by Christopher Paul Curtis

1930's

First A New Home

Bud and "Bugs" (Jerry Clark) were told they have homes and Bud's is with the Amos' Family.

Next Escape from the Amos's

The Amos' keep Bud in their shed, but Bud eventually escapes the shed.

Next Reunited!

Bud and "Bugs" find each other outside of the library after running away, and then find themselves in a "cardboard city".

Act II

Next Separated Again!

At the "cardboard city" Bud and "Bugs" are waiting for a train to go to Illinois, but Bud doesn't make the train. We don't hear from "Bugs" again in this story.

Next Where Am I?

Bud decides to walk to New Albany, but on the way he gets found by a car, Bud got in the car and fell asleep and woke up in a hospital.

Act III

Next Family Reunion

Bud gets to New Albany at his "Dads" house, and his "Dad" is in a band, Bud makes good friends with the bandmates, but Bud finds out his "Dad" is his Grandpa.

Last A Fitting End

One of the bandmates gave Bud a trumpet, and Bud joined the band.
